ASUNTHA FLEMING




Tennis Athlete & Coach


Raised on clay, Asuntha has been competing since childhood. She represented her state and carved out a path to play collegiate tennis in the United States, where resilience became her signature. Today, she trains athletes of all levels with a commitment to growth and a goal to bring out the best in them.

Her journey reflects the same values she teaches; patience, consistency, and composure under pressure. What began as a love for the game has become a lifelong pursuit of progress, both on and off the court.
